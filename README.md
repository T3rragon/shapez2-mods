# UltraBelt

UltraBelt adds in belt crossings as new buildings, allowing much tighter and more optimized platforms!

This mod is inspired by [Shapez Industries](https://mod.io/g/shapez/m/shapez-industries#description), and their belt crossings. Huge shout ot to them for the idea and implimentation in their Shapez 1 mod.

As of 24/5/26, there are 3 buildings:
- Belt Bridge, that crosses 2 straight belt paths
- Belt Weave In and Belt Weave Out, that crosses 2 corner belt paths in their respective orientations

There will be space belt variants in the future, along with more ways to manipulate belt flow.

> Code snippets for this mod taken from [MoreLogic](https://github.com/Raphdf201/shapez2-moreLogic) and [FourWaySplitterMod](https://github.com/SpicyDrk/Shapez2-Mods)

# More mod ideas

## Billboards

Large platforms that accept an input and showcases it, even when the player is zoomed out.

The idea for this mod is to add decal, flow, and possible visual explaination for larger factories.

## ShapeMerge

Combine shapes to get new shape types.
Directly taken from [Shapez Industries](https://mod.io/g/shapez/m/shapez-industries#description), but with a more consistent art direction and possibilities.
